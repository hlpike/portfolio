Motion Graphics: D&D and Empathetic Storytelling

Final, IML 230: Fundamentals of Media Design

            For my final project in IML 230: Fundamentals of Media Design, I created a motion-graphics video on a subject I care greatly about: tabletop role-playing. As a media arts major, I believe that tabletop RPGs are a fantastic storytelling medium in their own right, and I set out to prove it with this video, as well as demonstrate my motion graphics skills after a semester of Illustrator and After Effects learning.

April 2017

Programs Used:

Adobe Illustrator, After Effects
