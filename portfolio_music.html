The Mind of Hayley Pike through Songwriting

Final Project, IML 288: Critical Thinking and Procedural Media
            For my final project in IML 288: Critical Thinking and Procedural Media, I was tasked with making a Processing project that communicated a personal narrative through the structures of computational media, i.e, media that parameterizes, visualizes, simulates. I decided to document my love of music and personal songwriting through "interview" and music clips, as well as a system that dynamically transformed the songs into a visual format.

April 2017

Programs Used:

Java, Processing
