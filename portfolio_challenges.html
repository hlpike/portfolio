Weekly Illustrator Challenges

Project, IML 230: Fundamentals of Media Design

            During my spring 2017 semester class IML 230: Fundamentals of Media Design, I started an Adobe Illustrator Challenges series to practice my skills. These are weekly challenges designed to practice new aspects of the system and stretch my design ideation skills. After the class continued I have continued with monthly challenges in both Illustrator and Photoshop.
