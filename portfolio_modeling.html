3D Modeling/Worldbuilding Final

Final Project, IML 354: Intro to 3D Modeling

            For my final in my modeling class, I took a worldbuilding idea I had been developing for the semester– a sailboat powered by music instead of wind– and created a 3D environment around it in both Maya (using the Arnold render engine) and in Unity (which allowed it to be interactive). I created both the musical sailboat and its boathouse, researching the intricacies of boathouses and what it would mean for that environment to have this fantastical object instead of a conventional boat. I then made high-quality renders of the inside of the boathouse in Maya, and, practicing my modeling, sound, and interactive design skills, expanded on the setting in Unity. My goal for both pieces was evoking a certain mood through the lighting, sound, and interaction as much as my modeling skills.

January 2018-May 2018

Programs Used:

Maya, Unity 3D
