Micro/Macro Plastic in the Pacific Ocean

Systems, Space, and Time Project
            In this project, I created a series of information visualizations to bring attention to talk about the flow of plastic and other human waste into the Pacific Ocean. During the design process, I decided to try out a paper prototype for user experience testing. While the user navigates through videos and visuals about the subject, the program simulates the dumping of plastic in the ocean in real time, which the user can try to manipulate through placing more or less recycling centers/landfills in the state of California.

March 2017

Programs Used:
