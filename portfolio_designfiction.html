Blue Uncertain

Design Fiction Pitch Deck
October 2018

            I had the opportunity to pitch a project to the USC School of Cinematic Arts Dean of Research on a VR/AR design fiction. Design fiction is designed to spark the imagination for future technologies and apply critical thinking to its supposed ramifications. I combined themes of ubiquitous surveillence and AR to make a cyberpunk-dystopian story. It raises questions of agency, perception, and the role of the state that we may actually have to grapple with if this technology becomes widespread.
